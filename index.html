How to use the map
(someone dumb enough to with you is your responsibility)
This simple website lets you search Ireland for movie locations near you. Just click a marker on the map to get the information for a movie that was shot there. Scroll down the page to see the movie details and promotional poster or watch the trailer. Over the years, the Irish landscape has been featured in films as itself of course, but also posing as a foreign location (the beaches of Normandy in Saving Private Ryan) or as a flight of fancy (the Cliffs of Insanity in The Princess Bride). The list of locations is a work in progress. If you think a move should be added, contact us using the form and we'll add it in. You can move around and zoom the map as you normally would. Maybe it helps you plan a movie-lovers day out!
