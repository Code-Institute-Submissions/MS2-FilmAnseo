How to use the map

(someone dumb enough to go with you is your responsibility)

The map shows locations in Ireland where famous or award-winning films have been shot -

or at least had some scenes shot!


To get started, click a marker to get the movie name and basic location info.

Click the weather icon to see more weather info for the selected location today.

For movie info, scroll down to see more details or watch the trailer.


Maybe it helps you plan a movie-lovers day out!
